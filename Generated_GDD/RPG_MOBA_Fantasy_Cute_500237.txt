TOP LEVEL GENRE: RPG
GENRE: MOBA
SUB GENRE: Time Management
VISUALS: Third Person
THEME 1: Fantasy
THEME 2: Cute
FEATURES: Team-Based
PLAYERS: Multiplayer
ASSESSMENT: Addictive

==========

The objective of the game is to lead your team to victory by managing your time and resources wisely while defeating the enemy team. The game is set in a fantasy environment with cute characters. The unique selling points of the game are its team-based gameplay and its addictive nature.
