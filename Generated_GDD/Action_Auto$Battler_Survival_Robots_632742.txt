Auto Battler: Survival Craft 

GAME OVERVIEW: Auto Battler: Survival Craft is a fast-paced, open world survival game in which players must scavenge for resources and battling robots in order to survive. Environments are procedurally generated, providing a different challenge each time you play. Additionally, the game features resource management as players must carefully balance their use of resources in order to survive.
