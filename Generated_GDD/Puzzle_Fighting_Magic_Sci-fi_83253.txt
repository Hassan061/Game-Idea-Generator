The title of the game is "EARTH". 

GAME OVERVIEW: EARTH is a puzzle fighting game with a tactical first-person perspective. The objective of the game is to hack and slash your way through opponents using magic and sci-fi abilities. The game loop is simple: win fights to progress. The game environment is set on a post-apocalyptic earth, where characters must compete in a tournament to save the planet. The unique selling points of the game are its combination of genres, visuals, and themes.
