Dragons and Swords 

GAME OVERVIEW: Dragons and swords is a multiplayer, team-based, beat-em-up game set in an open world environment. The objective of the game is to defeat the other team by using a variety of weapons and fighting styles. The game loop consists of players fighting each other in teams of up to four players. The game environment is an online world with a variety of landscapes and settings. The characters are anime-inspired dragons that can transform into human form. The game's unique selling points include its team-based gameplay, its wide range of weapons and fighting styles, and its anime-inspired dragon characters.
