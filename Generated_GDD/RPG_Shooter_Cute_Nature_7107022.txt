PLAYERS: Multiplayer
ASSESSMENT: Emotional

==========

Cute Nature Combat is a top-down action roguelike shooter with cute visuals and a nature theme. The objective of the game is to combat other players in a multiplayer environment. The game loop consists of shooting and Dodging bullets while trying to kill the other players. The game environment is set in a nature theme with trees, bushes, and flowers. The characters are animals that have been anthropomorphized. The game's unique selling points are its cute visuals, nature theme, and combat system.
