TOP LEVEL GENRE: RPG
GENRE: Auto Battler
SUB GENRE: Hack and Slash
VISUALS: First-Person
THEME 1: Demons
THEME 2: Mythology
FEATURES: Crafting
PLAYERS: Single player
ASSESSMENT: Emotional

==========

"Mythical Battles: The Search for the Demon Lord" is an action-packed RPG with a hack-and-slash gameplay style. You take control of a brave hero on a journey to vanquish the Demon Lord, crafting powerful weapons and armor along the way to help you in your fight. With beautiful visuals and an emotionally engaging story, this game is sure to keep you hooked until the very end!
