Dante's Infernal Inferno 

GAME OVERVIEW: Dante's Infernal Inferno is a party-based looter shooter set in a top-down, visually stunning world inspired by anime. Hack and slash your way through hordes of demons to reach the ultimate objective: defeating Dante himself. With multiplayer support, gorgeous graphics, and an emotional soundtrack, this game is sure to be a hit with fans of the genre.
