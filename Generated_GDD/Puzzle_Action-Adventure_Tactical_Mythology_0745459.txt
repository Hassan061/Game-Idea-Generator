The title of this game is "Ruins of Atlantis." 

GAME OVERVIEW: This is a puzzle game with an action-adventure wind, set in a top-down perspective. The objective is to loot as much treasure as possible from the ruins of the lost city of Atlantis. Players will take on the role of one of several classes, each with their own strengths and weaknesses, in order to survive and prosper in this hostile environment. With its unique class system and mythological theme, Ruins of Atlantis promises to be an epic gaming experience.
